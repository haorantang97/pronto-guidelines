<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Pronto Community Guidelines</span></p>

<p class="p2"><span class="s2">Preamble: Platform Advocacy and Interaction Consensus</span></p>

<p class="p3"><span class="s3">Beyond the strict compliance baselines, the platform establishes the following interaction principles designed to minimize social friction for creators:</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Minimalist Communication and Directness:</span><span class="s3"> Discard cumbersome online social etiquette and unnecessary mutual pleasantries. The platform encourages utilizing standardized tools to initiate invitations directly upon a mutual match. Skip the meaningless preamble and get straight to the point.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Offline Priority and Genuine Presence:</span><span class="s3"> Online matching is merely the catalyst for connection. The platform advocates shifting the focus of interaction entirely to the real world. Establish high-signal-to-noise connections through genuine physical presence and face-to-face meetings to discuss artwork and concepts.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Work-Driven and Efficiency First:</span><span class="s3"> Abandon excessive self-packaging and social maneuvering. The platform encourages users to discard conventional social "hesitation" when encountering creators they appreciate. Do not miss the opportunity to connect due to unnecessary social reservations; ensure the motive remains strictly focused on the work itself.</span></p>

<p class="p5"><span class="s2">1. Account Access and Copyright Regulations</span></p>

<p class="p3"><span class="s3">This platform operates on a strict manual review and approval basis. Users must own the full copyright or possess the legal right to use all artworks they display.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Data Limitations &amp; Anti-AI Scraping:</span><span class="s3"> User data belongs solely to the user. The platform does not use, authorize, or sell any user data (including artwork images, text profiles, and interaction records) for the training of any Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) models.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">AI Creation Transparency:</span><span class="s3"> The platform permits the display of high-quality AI-assisted or AI-generated works. However, if a work contains AI-generated elements, the user </span><span class="s2">must</span><span class="s3"> explicitly label it using system tags upon upload. Concealing AI involvement or falsely presenting AI-generated images as traditional media will result in the immediate revocation of account access.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Copyright Dispute Mechanism:</span><span class="s3"> Art theft and plagiarism are strictly prohibited. The platform enforces a "Notice and Takedown" mechanism for copyright infringement reports. Upon notification, the reported party must provide source files (e.g., screenshots of layered files) to prove originality. Failure to provide valid proof will result in the mandatory removal of the work and potential account suspension.</span></p>

<p class="p5"><span class="s2">2. Content Moderation and NSFW Mechanisms</span></p>

<p class="p3"><span class="s3">The platform utilizes a combination of automated systems and manual patrols to strictly enforce the baseline compliance requirements of app stores.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">NSFW Content Classification:</span><span class="s3"> For legal images involving artistic nudity or human anatomy, users </span><span class="s2">must</span><span class="s3"> proactively check the NSFW option upon upload to apply a system-level blur. Malicious circumvention of this mechanism (e.g., uploading sensitive images as standard images) will result in account suspension.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Zero Tolerance for Pornography and Violations:</span><span class="s3"> Pure pornography is strictly prohibited. Content involving child exploitation, terrorism, extreme violence, gore, and hate speech (based on race, nationality, sexual orientation, gender identity, etc.) is strictly forbidden. Verified violations will result in an immediate, permanent account ban and will be reported to relevant law enforcement agencies as required.</span></p>

<p class="p5"><span class="s2">3. Social Interaction and Offline Safety</span></p>

<p class="p3"><span class="s3">This application does not provide a built-in instant messaging feature. Users establish contact using private contact information unlocked upon a mutual match, utilizing a standardized in-app invitation tool.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Public Venue Principle:</span><span class="s3"> All initial offline meetings initiated through this application </span><span class="s2">must</span><span class="s3"> occur in public venues (e.g., cafes, art galleries). Invitations to private residences, studios, or other non-public areas are strictly prohibited.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Prohibition of Commercial Promotion:</span><span class="s3"> The sole purpose of establishing connections is artistic exchange. Utilizing contact information obtained through the platform for any commercial purposes (e.g., selling courses, marketing art supplies) is strictly prohibited.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Harassment and Offline Accountability:</span><span class="s3"> Contact information unlocked post-match is exclusively for issuing meeting invitations. If an invitation is unanswered or declined, persistent messaging is strictly prohibited. If harassment or violations occur during an offline meeting facilitated by this platform, users must immediately utilize the in-app "Report" function. The platform retains the right to retroactively track and permanently ban accounts that exploit this mechanism for malicious offline behavior.</span></p>

<p class="p5"><span class="s2">4. Mandatory Platform Intervention Tools</span></p>

<p class="p3"><span class="s3">To ensure user safety and maintain community standards, the platform mandates and enforces the following mechanisms:</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Block:</span><span class="s3"> Users may utilize this function at any time to unilaterally sever all interactions and visibility with a specific account.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Report:</span><span class="s3"> Users can report violating content or accounts. The platform's moderation team will complete a manual review and take appropriate action within [Insert Your Timeframe, e.g., 24 hours].</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Penalty Execution:</span><span class="s3"> The platform reserves the absolute right to evaluate account status and revoke the access privileges of violating users at any time without prior notice.</span></p>

<p class="p5"><span class="s2">5. Community Purity and Account Validity</span></p>

<p class="p3"><span class="s3">The platform maintains a high signal-to-noise ratio and ensures effective interaction rates through its strict manual review mechanism.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Creator Exclusivity:</span><span class="s3"> The platform does not offer "guest" or "view-only" accounts. Every account visible in the matching pool has passed a manual verification regarding work quality and genuine creator identity.</span></p>

<p class="p4"><span class="s3">&bull; </span><span class="s2">Intervention for Abnormal Interaction:</span><span class="s3"> The platform imposes no mandatory limits on daily activity. However, if an account is repeatedly reported for "fake socializing" (including but not limited to: frequently matching but deliberately refusing to communicate, or severely misrepresenting profile information), the moderation team will initiate an investigation. The platform reserves the right to unconditionally revoke community access for accounts confirmed to be abusing the matching mechanism or lacking genuine intent for communication.</span></p>
