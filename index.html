Pronto Community Guidelines

Preamble: Platform Advocacy and Interaction Consensus

Beyond the strict compliance baselines, the platform establishes the following interaction principles designed to minimize social friction for creators:

• Minimalist Communication and Directness: Discard cumbersome online social etiquette and unnecessary mutual pleasantries. The platform encourages utilizing standardized tools to initiate invitations directly upon a mutual match. Skip the meaningless preamble and get straight to the point.

• Offline Priority and Genuine Presence: Online matching is merely the catalyst for connection. The platform advocates shifting the focus of interaction entirely to the real world. Establish high-signal-to-noise connections through genuine physical presence and face-to-face meetings to discuss artwork and concepts.

• Work-Driven and Efficiency First: Abandon excessive self-packaging and social maneuvering. The platform encourages users to discard conventional social "hesitation" when encountering creators they appreciate. Do not miss the opportunity to connect due to unnecessary social reservations; ensure the motive remains strictly focused on the work itself.

1. Account Access and Copyright Regulations

This platform operates on a strict manual review and approval basis. Users must own the full copyright or possess the legal right to use all artworks they display.

• Data Limitations & Anti-AI Scraping: User data belongs solely to the user. The platform does not use, authorize, or sell any user data (including artwork images, text profiles, and interaction records) for the training of any Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) models.

• AI Creation Transparency: The platform permits the display of high-quality AI-assisted or AI-generated works. However, if a work contains AI-generated elements, the user must explicitly label it using system tags upon upload. Concealing AI involvement or falsely presenting AI-generated images as traditional media will result in the immediate revocation of account access.

• Copyright Dispute Mechanism: Art theft and plagiarism are strictly prohibited. The platform enforces a "Notice and Takedown" mechanism for copyright infringement reports. Upon notification, the reported party must provide source files (e.g., screenshots of layered files) to prove originality. Failure to provide valid proof will result in the mandatory removal of the work and potential account suspension.

2. Content Moderation and NSFW Mechanisms

The platform utilizes a combination of automated systems and manual patrols to strictly enforce the baseline compliance requirements of app stores.

• NSFW Content Classification: For legal images involving artistic nudity or human anatomy, users must proactively check the NSFW option upon upload to apply a system-level blur. Malicious circumvention of this mechanism (e.g., uploading sensitive images as standard images) will result in account suspension.

• Zero Tolerance for Pornography and Violations: Pure pornography is strictly prohibited. Content involving child exploitation, terrorism, extreme violence, gore, and hate speech (based on race, nationality, sexual orientation, gender identity, etc.) is strictly forbidden. Verified violations will result in an immediate, permanent account ban and will be reported to relevant law enforcement agencies as required.

3. Social Interaction and Offline Safety

This application does not provide a built-in instant messaging feature. Users establish contact using private contact information unlocked upon a mutual match, utilizing a standardized in-app invitation tool.

• Public Venue Principle: All initial offline meetings initiated through this application must occur in public venues (e.g., cafes, art galleries). Invitations to private residences, studios, or other non-public areas are strictly prohibited.

• Prohibition of Commercial Promotion: The sole purpose of establishing connections is artistic exchange. Utilizing contact information obtained through the platform for any commercial purposes (e.g., selling courses, marketing art supplies) is strictly prohibited.

• Harassment and Offline Accountability: Contact information unlocked post-match is exclusively for issuing meeting invitations. If an invitation is unanswered or declined, persistent messaging is strictly prohibited. If harassment or violations occur during an offline meeting facilitated by this platform, users must immediately utilize the in-app "Report" function. The platform retains the right to retroactively track and permanently ban accounts that exploit this mechanism for malicious offline behavior.

4. Mandatory Platform Intervention Tools

To ensure user safety and maintain community standards, the platform mandates and enforces the following mechanisms:

• Block: Users may utilize this function at any time to unilaterally sever all interactions and visibility with a specific account.

• Report: Users can report violating content or accounts. The platform's moderation team will complete a manual review and take appropriate action within [Insert Your Timeframe, e.g., 24 hours].

• Penalty Execution: The platform reserves the absolute right to evaluate account status and revoke the access privileges of violating users at any time without prior notice.

5. Community Purity and Account Validity

The platform maintains a high signal-to-noise ratio and ensures effective interaction rates through its strict manual review mechanism.

• Creator Exclusivity: The platform does not offer "guest" or "view-only" accounts. Every account visible in the matching pool has passed a manual verification regarding work quality and genuine creator identity.

• Intervention for Abnormal Interaction: The platform imposes no mandatory limits on daily activity. However, if an account is repeatedly reported for "fake socializing" (including but not limited to: frequently matching but deliberately refusing to communicate, or severely misrepresenting profile information), the moderation team will initiate an investigation. The platform reserves the right to unconditionally revoke community access for accounts confirmed to be abusing the matching mechanism or lacking genuine intent for communication.
